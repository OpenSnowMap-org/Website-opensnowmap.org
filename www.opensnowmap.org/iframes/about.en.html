The trails

  On last update of this website's database(**update**), we had **length**km mapped cross-country trails!
  All of these data and the background map are provided by OpenStreetMap, a free editable map of the whole world. It is made by people like you. These people covered nordic ski pistes (and roads, path, ...) with their GPS or their smartphones to record their track before uploading and trace them in this map, other have used open satellite imagery.
  More than 300'000 people have contributed.
To contribute, and map your favorite resort, it's easy: Beginner's guide.

The website

  This website is built on open-source tools. The source code is available here: pistes-nordiques.org.tar. You can use it freely, under a GPL license for code and CC-BY-SA for content.


Snow cover

  Low zoom map show last 8-days Modis Snow cover, current data from **modis-update**.
Hall, Dorothy K., George A. Riggs, and Vincent V. Salomonson. 2006, updated weekly. MODIS/Terra Snow Cover 8-day L3 Global 500m Grid V005. Boulder, Colorado USA: National Snow and Ice Data Center. Digital media.


Updates

  The server use a local planet file updated with hourly diffs (but not every hour) to update the trail database.
  By now, we have one update per day, around 4:00 am UTC.
